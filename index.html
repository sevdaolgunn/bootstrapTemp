Yedigöller-BOLU

The Black Sea region begins east of Istanbul in Bolu, whose centerpiece is Yedigoller (Yedigöller) or Seven Lakes National Park. With a climate that produces multi-colored forests of oak, alder, pine and hazelnut, the park entices visitors to travel through all four seasons of the year within the course of a short drive. The view is amazingly enchanting from Kapankaya peak in the Yedigoller National Park. While you do your camping daily picnics, trekking and photographing you could accommodate yourself in bungalows and guesthouses which are in the national park.
